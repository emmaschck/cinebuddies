Vision

We believe cinema is more than entertainment. It creates moments of exchange and connection between people.

CineBuddies exists to encourage more human cultural experiences around film.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a bridge between cinema-goers and arthouse cinemas by facilitating connections based on shared movie experiences.

Values

Human connection • Cultural discovery • Independent cinema • Community
