Our Vision

Making cinema social again.

CineBuddies aims to transform any film screening into the beginning of a new connection. Watching a movie should not end when the credits roll — it should continue through spontaneous conversations, emotional reactions, and shared interpretations.

By encouraging in-person exchanges around films, we want to create more welcoming and human cinema experiences for everyone.

Our Mission

Building a digital bridge between cinema-goers and arthouse cinemas.

CineBuddies facilitates connections between users based on shared movie tastes and specific showtimes.

Our mission is to support independent cinemas by helping audiences discover films together, meet naturally after screenings, and build local communities around cinema culture.
