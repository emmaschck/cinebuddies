Our Vision

Reimagining the social experience of cinema.

We believe cinema is more than entertainment. A screening can become a moment of exchange, curiosity, and connection between people who would otherwise never meet.

CineBuddies exists to encourage more human and more social cultural experiences around film.

Our Mission

Building bridges between cinema-goers and independent cinemas.

Our mission is to provide a digital bridge between cinema-goers and arthouse cinemas by facilitating connections between users based on shared movie tastes and specific showtimes.

We aim to encourage richer cultural experiences and strengthen local cinema communities through human interaction around film.
